/about.me

Hey there! I'm Klejdi (I go by Klej), a student who's all about tech, creativity, and learning by breaking things and fixing them again.

I mess around with Linux (way too much), build cool stuff using open source tools, and customize desktops like it's an art form. Whether it's tiling window managers, Conky setups, or making old-school systems feel brand new — I'm probably trying it, tweaking it, or writing about it here.

I'm also diving into programming, system engineering, and the kind of hands-on IT work that most people only do once they're already in the field. Why wait?

Outside the screen? I draw too — mostly characters, poses, and the occasional flower. Art and tech don't clash for me — they click. While I'm a traditional artist now, I'm excited to venture into digital art with my upcoming XPPen Deco 01 V3 tablet, using Krita on desktop and ibisPaint on mobile devices.

This site is my lab, my notebook, and my open book. It's where I share what I'm building, what I'm learning, and what I wish someone had told me earlier. If any of it helps you learn, explore, or even just mess around smarter — mission accomplished.
